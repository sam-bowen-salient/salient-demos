The Broken Industry

You hired a security company. So why are you the one managing them?

"I can never reach anyone."

You call about a no-show and get a voicemail tree. Your account manager changed three times this year. Nobody knows your name.

"Guards just don't show up."

Industry turnover exceeds 100%. The guard you trained last month? Already gone. No-shows are the single most common complaint in private security.

"They won the contract and forgot about us."

Big firms chase volume. Once you sign, you become a row in a spreadsheet of 300,000 employees. Quality drops. Nobody notices but you.

"I have zero visibility into my site."

No real-time reporting. No dashboards. You find out about incidents after the fact — if at all. You're paying for security you can't verify.

"Every guard is different."

Constant rotation. Your officers don't know the building, the people, or the protocols. You're retraining someone new every week.

"One-size-fits-all security."

Same cookie-cutter playbook whether you're a hospital or a warehouse. No customization, no thought — just warm bodies at the door.
